🌿PCOD-Specific Protocol Rules
Biofeedback Adjustments
🔴
Acne flare-up
Reduce paneer/dairy that week. Switch to tofu, eggs, chicken. Increase zinc via pumpkin seeds. Dairy sensitivity or insulin spike is likely cause.
🗓
Period delayed
Slightly increase complex carbs (+30g quinoa/millet). Low energy availability or stress is the likely cause.
💧
Luteal phase (days 15–28)
Expect 1–2 kg water retention. Do NOT panic or reduce calories. This is normal and temporary.
😴
Energy crash / fatigue
Check sleep quality first. Add 1 extra complex carb serving. Consider B12 check if persistent.
